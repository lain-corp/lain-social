# Privacy Policy for Lain Social Bot

**Effective Date:** November 19, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 19, 2025

## Overview

Lain Social Bot ("we," "our," or "the bot") is an automated social media posting application that shares Lain Iwakura content from Serial Experiments Lain across multiple social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our bot.

## Information We Collect

### Automated Data Collection
- **Platform Interaction Data:** When the bot posts content, we may receive standard platform responses (post IDs, success/failure status)
- **Configuration Data:** Environment variables and settings needed for platform authentication (stored locally)
- **Usage Logs:** Basic operational logs for debugging and monitoring bot performance

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Personal User Data:** We do not collect personal information from users who view or interact with bot posts
- **User Content:** We do not collect, store, or analyze user-generated content or comments
- **Tracking Data:** We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies
- **Private Messages:** We do not access or store private messages or direct communications

## How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely for:
- **Bot Operation:** Posting content to configured social media platforms
- **Technical Maintenance:** Debugging issues and ensuring reliable operation
- **Platform Compliance:** Meeting API requirements and platform terms of service

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** All configuration and credentials are stored locally on the host system
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not store user data or personal information in cloud services
- **API Credentials:** Platform API tokens and credentials are stored securely using environment variables
- **Content Storage:** Images and generated content are stored locally and are not transmitted except when posting

## Third-Party Platforms

This bot integrates with multiple social media platforms:
- Twitter/X
- Reddit
- Instagram
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- Discord
- Telegram
- WhatsApp
- Signal
- YouTube
- Bluesky

Each platform has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. When the bot posts content, that content becomes subject to the respective platform's privacy policy and terms of service.

## AI Content Generation

- **AI Services:** We may use AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, OpenRouter) to generate captions and comments
- **Content Processing:** Images and generated text are processed by AI services according to their privacy policies
- **No Personal Data:** Only Lain-themed content and generated commentary are processed, not personal user data

## User Rights and Control

### For Platform Users:
- You can block, mute, or unfollow the bot account on any platform
- You can report the bot if you believe it violates platform policies
- The bot does not collect data about users who interact with its posts

### For Administrators:
- Bot administrators can modify or delete posted content
- All bot configuration and data is under administrator control
- The bot can be disabled or removed at any time

## Data Retention

- **Operational Logs:** Basic logs are retained temporarily for troubleshooting purposes
- **Posted Content:** Content posted to platforms is subject to each platform's retention policies
- **Configuration Data:** Stored locally until manually removed by administrators

## Children's Privacy

This bot is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through interaction with the bot, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the bot after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

## Compliance

This bot strives to comply with applicable privacy laws including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Platform-specific privacy requirements
